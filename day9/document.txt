Andolasoft is a software company that builds web and mobile applications.

Employees receive 18 days of paid leave every year.

The password reset process requires users to click the Forgot Password link on the login page.

Two-factor authentication is mandatory for all administrator accounts.

Office hours are Monday to Thursday from 9 AM to 6 PM. The office is closed on Fridays.

New employees must complete onboarding within their first 30 days.

IT support requests can be submitted through support.andolasoft.com.

All code changes must be reviewed by a senior developer before merging.

Employees should regularly update their passwords for security.

Company laptops should not be shared with anyone outside the organization.

All confidential information must remain inside the company network.

Training sessions are held every month for all employees.
